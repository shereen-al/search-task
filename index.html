Football Club
Horsham Football Club

Having opened in 2019, the Horsham Football Club boasts excellent, state-of-the-art facilities that are the envy of many clubs at our level of football. The Camping World Community Stadium can hold almost 2,000 spectators and includes a great clubhose, licensed bar, plus:

Food and refreshments - we are able to provide a great range of hot and cold food and refreshments from our kitchen. On matchdays the food service is accessed at the window close to the turnstiles. Typically, we offer burgers, hot dogs, chips and other hot options, as well as a range of crisps, snacks and soft drinks.

In addition, our beach hut bar located next to the main stand serves a more limited range of alcoholic and soft drinks, along with crisps, snacks and hot food, plus our horse box, in between the club house and main stand serves hot drinks, soft drinks, crisps and chocolate bars.

Main stand - our beautiful main stand has seating for 300 spectators and also a platform that can accommodate four wheelchair users, plus, we have 3 covered, stepped terracing sections. The Octotech Stand behind the goal at the far end of the ground provides cover along around half the length of that end and on the side opposite the main stand, we have two covered standing sections either side of the dugouts. Again these cover about half the length of that side of the ground. There's ample cover for spectators on a rainy day.

Disabled access - our stadium has full disabled access. This includes a platform for viewing the match with unrestricted views, for up to four wheelchair users, and disabled toilet facilities both in the clubhouse and in the outside toilet block.

Pitches - our main pitch is a state-of-the-art 3G football surface. The beauty of an artificial pitch is that it delivers a consistent surface conducive to passing football and there will never be a postponement for heavy rain.

We also have the Jack Scrase Community Pitch which is suitable for training or youth football.

Both pitches are available to hire when not in use by our first team or youth teams. For more details, please visit the venue hire section.

Nature Reserves FOWLNR

FOWLNR
Warnham Local Nature Reserve
Warnham Road
Horsham
RH12 2RA

FOWLNR stands for Friends Of Warnham Local Nature Reserve.

The Warnham reserve is a treasured asset of Horsham with many different habitats supporting a wide range of fauna and flora.

The 17-acre Millpond with its reedbeds, islands and marginal vegetation dominates this 92-acre site. Renown romantic poet Percy Bysshe Shelley is said to have learned to sail on this Millpond in his youth.

There is ample free parking, numerous trails and several hides to watch the wildlife from, plus, the Visitor Centre has an exhibition room and disabled access toilets with baby changing facilities.

The Herons Rest cafe serves drinks and snacks.

Sorry, dogs are not allowed on the Nature Reserve.

01403 756 238
